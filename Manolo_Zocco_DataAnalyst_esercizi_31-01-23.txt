Esercizi Data Analyst 31/01/2023
Esempio di 3 dataset strutturati

Car Evaluation Data Set: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/car+evaluation

	Attributi: doors, persons, safety.

	Identificativi:
		Doors: 2,3,4,5 more.
		Persons: 2,4,5, more.
		Safety: Low, med, high.


Student Alcohol Consumption Data Set: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/uciml/student-alcohol-consumption

	Attributi: school, sex, age, address.

	Identificativi:
		School: GP (Gabriel Pereira), MS (Mousinho da Silveira)
		Sex: student's sex (binary: 'F' - female or 'M' - male)
		Age: student's age (numeric: from 15 to 22)
		Address: student's home address type (binary: 'U' - urban or 'R' - rural)
		
	
The Last Of Us Tweets Data Set: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ulisesmontoyacanales/the-last-of-us-tweets

	Attributi: id, author_id, text, created_at.

	Identificativi:
		Id: tweet id
		author_id: author twtter id
		Text: tweet
		created_at: date the tweet was created on
